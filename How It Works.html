"Phase 1:Skill Up(Gaining the Tools)"

1.Application and Screening:A motivated individual applies on our website. We review their application to ensure genuinity

2.Training Selection:The accepted individual chooses a high-demand skill.

3.Intensive Workshops: The beneficiary attends our free, hands-training to master their choosen skill.

"Phase 2:Getting the business started"

1.Business Planning:Mentors help create a simple and realistic business plan.

2.Funding Disbursement:The beneficiary receives their micro-loan or seed capital for essential start_up costs(tools,materials).

3.Go Live:The new entrepreneur launches their small business.

"Phase 3:Sustain and Grow(sucess ensuring"

1.Financial Coaching:The entrepreneur attends workshops on budgeting, saving and pricing.

2.Mentorship Network:The business owner is paired with experienced volunteer mentor for the first year.

3.Loan Repayment:Repayments begin by feeding the fund to help the next person in a given period of time.

4.Graduation of the entrepreneur:He/She graduates when the business is stable and their financial succes is achieved.
