The project aims to lift families out of extreme poverty by adressing financial exclusion and lack of employment of a generational microfinance fund for creation of small business and providing of vocational training as well as adressing the financial literacy .

Why Choose Future Builders

We believe sustainable change comes from within and many lack just two things to escape poverty and that is a skill and a start .Our project fills this gap, fostering self-reliance and economic independence within our community
