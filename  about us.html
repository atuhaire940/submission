ABOUT US

Founded in 1997, Future Builders was established to end poverty by turning Prof.Kanuuke Isa's vision of Microfinance into a globally recognised project.Today, we aim at deepening our impact by investing in women and moving 10 million families out of poverty by 2030 through our project

We are a group of people passionate about helping our neighbours who are struggling financially. We believe that poverty isnt just a lack of poverty isnt just a lack of effort; its often a lack of opportunity s]and essential tools. Think of us as a launchpad where we give people the boost they need to become self-reliant and financially stable

Our Vision: Future Builders

To create a future where every member of our community has the skills,
financial resources and opportunity to build stable, dignified life,
making extreme poverty a story of the past.

Mission

To empower motivate motivated individuals and families to achieve financial self reliance by providing free vocational training, accessible
micro-funding and sustainable financial coaching ensuring their economic success is permanent.

OUR GOAL

By 2040, we shall ensure that 2milliom women and their families leave poverty forever.

WHAT EXACTLY DO WE DO?

Our work is split into three simple, effective steps, ensuring the help is long-lasting:

STEP ONE

The knowlegde:We run free, hands-on workshops focused on modern, useful skills like digital marketing that can earn income.

STEP TWO

The starting Cash:We offer small loans or grants(seed capital)to help trainees buy equipments such as sewing machine, tools. The cashflow transforms
a skill into a real,operating small business.

STEP THREE

The sustainability:We teach simple, practical financial literacy like how to budget, save and price products. This ensures that the business doesnt just survive but grows.

Our promise to the community

Every donationand every loan repayment goes back into the revolving fund to help the next person and by supporting us, you are building an entire eco-system
that teaches, funds and sustains itself.
